Valorant

So, the second game I want to introduce to you guys is 'Valorant'.
This is a FPS ( First Person Shooter ) tactical - shooter game of 24 rounds.
First team to win 13 round wins the match.
This game has many maps like Pearl, Bind, Iceage, Haven, etc.
It also has many modes like Competitve, Unrated, Spike Rush, Replication, Escalation and many more!
Not all modes have 24 rounds, Replication has 10 rounds out of which, first team to win 5 rounds wins the match and many more....
My favourite maps are Haven and Iceage.
